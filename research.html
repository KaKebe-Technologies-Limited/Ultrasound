Research

Research is a critical component in the field of ultrasound, as it is through research that new discoveries and advancements are made. At Ultrasound Africa, we recognize the importance of research in the field of medical sonography and its impact on the delivery of high-quality healthcare.


Our intention is to conduct research studies that will provide a better understanding of the challenges facing healthcare in Africa, and to identify effective solutions that can improve patient outcomes. By doing so, we hope to contribute to the development of evidence-based practices that can be implemented by ultrasound practitioners in their clinical work.

We understand the challenges that students face in conducting research, especially in resource-limited settings. To support students in research, Ultrasound Africa will provide access to research materials and resources, as well as offer mentorship and guidance to students who are interested in conducting research studies.

Ultimately, our goal is to promote research as an essential aspect of professional development and to encourage ultrasound practitioners to actively engage in research activities. By promoting research, we believe that we can improve the quality of healthcare services and contribute to the development of evidence-based practices that benefit patients and healthcare providers.
