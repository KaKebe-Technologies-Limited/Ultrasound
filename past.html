Transcranial Doppler Ultrasound in Paediatric Sickle Cell Disease

Presenter: Ibrahim Ismail, Clinical Vascular Scientist
Join us for an insightful webinar with Clinical Vascular Scientist Ibrahim Ismail, as he explores the role of Transcranial Doppler Ultrasound (TCD) in the management of paediatric sickle cell disease (SCD). This comprehensive session will provide valuable information for healthcare professionals, researchers, and caregivers interested in understanding the applications of TCD in detecting and preventing cerebrovascular complications in children with SCD.

Key topics covered in this webinar include:

1. Overview of paediatric sickle cell disease and its impact on cerebrovascular health.
2. Introduction to Transcranial Doppler Ultrasound and its clinical applications.
3. The role of TCD in early detection of stroke risk in children with SCD.
4. Strategies for stroke prevention and management in paediatric SCD using TCD.
5. Exploration of imaging and non-imaging modalities in the assessment and management of SCD.
6. Case studies showcasing the effectiveness of TCD in improving patient outcomes. Don't miss this opportunity to expand your knowledge and enhance patient care for children with sickle cell disease.

Register now and stay informed about the latest advancements in TCD technology and its potential to transform SCD management, as well as other imaging and non-imaging modalities that can be leveraged in the field.
