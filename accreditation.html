Accreditation, Certification and Protocols

Our vision is to become your trusted partner for ultrasound Accreditation, Certification, and Protocol Development. Our goal is to offer high-quality services to individual ultrasound practitioners, teaching and healthcare institutions.

Our accreditation and certification programmes aim at promoting and maintaining highest standards of clinical practice in sonography, ensuring proficiency and professionalism in line with internationally accepted standards of practice.

Ultrasound Africa, in collaboration with regulatory bodies and national ultrasound professional organisations, aims to offer comprehensive accreditation programs to healthcare and teaching institutions. This joint effort will ensure that ultrasound departments meet the highest standards of clinical practice and have the requisite amenities to provide exceptional ultrasound services.

By fostering excellence in ultrasound practice across the continent, we make a lasting impact on healthcare service delivery and ultrasound education.

We have established a committee of ultrasound professionals responsible for developing and updating practice policies, guidelines, protocols, and Standard Operating Procedures (SoPs) for ultrasound departments. These resources ensure consistent delivery of high-quality ultrasound services in accordance with the latest research and best practices.

Explore our Accreditation, Certification, and Protocol Development services to enhance your professional development and contribute to the advancement of sonography.
