Welcome to Ultrasound Africa

Ultrasound Africa is a voluntary provider of Continuous Professional Development programmes and training in ultrasound to members of professional bodies and teaching institutions that are involved in the field of Radiology and Imaging. Our CPD and training sessions have impacted thousands of ultrasound practitioners since 2017.Our ultrasound research and outreach programmes are aimed at supporting quality healthcare delivery and informing policy in Africa, especially in resource constrained settings
The idea of supporting good quality healthcare delivery in low and middle income countries was born in 2017 by ultrasound practitioners in high income settings but with a background of low resource settings.We train qualified ultrasound practitioners in both basic and speciality sonography techniques.Our outreach programmes are designed to provide high quality ultrasound services voluntarily to low resource settings.

Facts

We have set up a vibrant committee of ultrasound practitioners that develop and update protocols and guidelines for ultrasound practice

Our specialist ultrasound educators are highly qualified and bring on board extensive experience in the field of ultrasound imaging.

We introduce new ultrasound imaging techniques to trainees in health facilities in resource limited settings

Through our CPD programs we equip professionals with the knowledge, practical skills, resources, and offer them the support they need to provide high quality patient care through ultrasound imaging. We have impacted thousands of ultrasound practitioners in the African region and beyond between 2017 and 2023

We aim at quality improvement of ultrasound practice by developing a vibrant community of practice in research, linking students and researchers through a central research hub and providing access to information and dissemination of research findings related to the field of ultrasound.
