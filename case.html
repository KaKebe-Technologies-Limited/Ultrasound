Monthly Case

Brief History

Forehead lump, mobile, soft and fluctuant, persistent for more than 1 year.

Solid echogenic mass between the periosteum and galea aponeurosis. It has a relatively flat base and smooth convex displacement of galea aponeurosis. No vascularity. Underlying bony cortex is smooth.

Diagnosis

Subgaleal lipoma.
